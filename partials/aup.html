Acceptable Use Policy

Guidelines and rules for utilizing RyzenNodes servers.

1. Prohibited Activities

You may not use RyzenNodes services to engage in the following activities:

  • Hosting malware, phishing sites, or engaging in any form of cyber-attack (DDoS).
  • Sending unsolicited bulk email (Spam).
  • Mining cryptocurrencies on shared resource plans.
  • Hosting content that violates intellectual property laws.

2. Resource Abuse

While our network ports are 5Gbps/10Gbps, bandwidth must be used fairly. Continuous, non-stop maximization of port capacity that harms other node users may result in automatic traffic shaping or bandwidth restriction.

3. Enforcement

Violations of this Acceptable Use Policy will result in immediate suspension or termination of your services without a refund. We cooperate fully with law enforcement requests regarding illegal activities.
